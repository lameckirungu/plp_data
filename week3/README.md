# Week 3 — Multi-Source Operational Data Pipeline

**Topic:** Sourcing, merging, and analysing data from three independent sources

---

## Research Question

> *Does hourly rainfall correlate with lower flow rate throughput at the plant?*

**Finding:** No. Pearson r = 0.130, p = 0.093 — the relationship is weak, positive,
and not statistically significant. Rainfall is not a driver of throughput variation
over this 7-day period.
